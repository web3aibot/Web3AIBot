Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-13

This Privacy Policy explains how Web3 AI Reply (“we”, “our”, “the Extension”) handles information when you use our Chrome extension on x.com / twitter.com.

Summary

  • The Extension processes only the data required to perform user-requested actions.
  • User preferences are stored locally using chrome.storage.
  • Some requests are sent to external APIs (AI and backend services) to provide functionality.
  • We do not sell personal data and do not collect passwords.

Information we process

1) User-provided content

  • Tweet URLs you paste or select.
  • Text you approve or submit for AI reply generation.

2) Public page context

  • Publicly visible tweet text and metadata on X/Twitter pages you interact with, only to generate replies or perform actions you explicitly request.

3) Local extension storage

  • Settings and preferences you configure in the Extension.
  • Stored locally via chrome.storage to persist behavior across sessions.

4) Account, credits, and payments (if enabled)

  • If login is enabled, an email address may be processed to identify your account.
  • If a credits system is used, credit balance and transaction status are stored to provide the service.

Information we do NOT collect

  • Your X/Twitter password.
  • Your private messages.
  • Your full browsing history.
  • Personal data for advertising or resale.

How we use information

  • To generate AI replies and perform actions you request.
  • To store your preferences and keep the Extension functional.
  • To prevent abuse, ensure stability, and debug errors.

Third-party services

Depending on enabled features, the Extension may communicate with:

  • Groq API (https://api.groq.com) for AI text generation.
  • Firebase / Google Cloud (*.cloudfunctions.net, *.firebaseapp.com) for backend services such as authentication, credits, and API endpoints.

These services process data only as required to deliver the Extension’s functionality and are governed by their own privacy policies.

Data retention

  • Local data: Stored on your device until you clear extension data or uninstall.
  • Backend data (if applicable): Retained only as long as necessary to provide the service and meet legal or security requirements.
  • Logs: Minimal operational logs may be kept temporarily for debugging and abuse prevention.

Security

We use HTTPS and standard security practices to protect data in transit and at rest. While no system is 100% secure, reasonable safeguards are applied.

Your choices

  • You may uninstall the Extension at any time.
  • You can clear stored settings via browser extension controls.
  • You may request deletion of account-related backend data by contacting us.

Changes to this policy
